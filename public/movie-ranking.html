My Top 3 Movies

They are mentioned below as of today

Scarface

Tony Montana and his close friend Manny, build a strong drug empire in Miami.
However as his power begins to grow, so does his ego and his enemies,
and his own paranoia begins to plague his empire.

Intersteller

When Earth becomes uninhabitable in the future, a farmer and ex-NASA pilot, Joseph Cooper, is tasked to pilot a spacecraft, along with a team of researchers,
to find a new planet for humans.

The Dark Knight

Batman has a new foe, the Joker, who is an accomplished criminal hell-bent on decimating
Gotham City. Together with Gordon and Harvey Dent, Batman struggles to thwart the Joker
before it is too late.
